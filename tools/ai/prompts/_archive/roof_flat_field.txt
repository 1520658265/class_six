A single flat concrete roof tile for a 1990s rural Chinese school building.

The tile should be:
- Weathered grey concrete surface with subtle texture
- Slight variations in tone (lighter patches where sun-bleached, darker where water-stained)
- Faint cracks or wear marks typical of aged concrete
- Seamlessly tileable on all four edges (no borders or frames)
- Flat perspective (top-down view, no 3D depth)

Technical requirements:
- 32×32 pixels
- Tileable pattern (edges must connect seamlessly when repeated)
- No shadows or highlights that would break tiling
- Uniform lighting across the entire tile
